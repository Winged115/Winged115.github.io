Welcome to the fan webpage for the Detroit Red Wings!

Fun Facts

The Detroit Red Wings were founded in 1926, and are one of the six original members of the NHL. The Red Wings have won more Stanley Cup championships than any other team based in the United States. Their current captain is Henrik Zetterberg. Their current head coach is Jeff Blashill.
